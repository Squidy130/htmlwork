Help at Awesome Furnitures

If you have questions about your order, our products, or how to navigate the site, visit our FAQ section or contact our support team via email or phone. We're here Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM.

How To Pay

We accept a variety of secure payment methods including Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, and mobile money. All transactions are encrypted to ensure your information is safe. You'll receive a confirmation email once your payment is succesful.

How to Get Your Delivery

Once your order is placed, our team will process it within 24 hours. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email. We also offer express and scheduled delivery options in select areas.
